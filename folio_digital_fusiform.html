Fusiform, Inc.

LEAD UX & UI DESIGN

A Startup Revolutionizing Orthotics Through Modularity

February 2016 - Present

Background

Objective

Optimize efficiency for orthotists and manufacturers, reducing waste and time, while ensuring increased patient comfort.

Understanding the User Base: Implications for Design Direction

Our user demographic consists of primarily middle-aged clinicians. From client meetings, the consistently expressed needs comprised the following: digitalized order forms, easily accessible and modifiable media uploads and patient records, design for efficiency, organization, readability, and clarity.

Responsibilities

  • Define and produce feature requirements for web and mobile properties, as well as a dynamic order management platform dedicated to streamlining the custom fabrication order process for orthotics
  • Develop process flows, wireframes, and mockups to effectively conceptualize design strategies
  • Produce style and help guides and provide thought leadership in constructing effective user-interaction across various digital assets for the company
  • Identify pain points in the user experience, develop more efficient ways to present data, transition between pages, and navigate the application
  • Communicate with developers and clients through an iterative design process
  • Further knowledge in front-end development for more fluidity in product development, pursuing projects to expand experience in these areas

Company Redesign & Branding

Rebuilding the Company Website

A place to showcase our company mission, product features, press, and updates. I first started by drafting process flows in Lucidchart to establish seamless navigation, moved to Sketch to create more extensive wireframes. Graphics, including the company logo and styleguide, were primarily designed in Adobe Illustrator. The final website was built using HTML/CSS.

Tools Used: Lucidchart, Sketch, Adobe Illustrator, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap

Building the Web Application: Feature

Order Forms

A web application for clinicians and fabricators to create, manage, and track orders. Our digital platform aims to increase efficiency in the orthotics industry with functions such as scan annotation, digital order forms, and seamless communication with our online comments.

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap

Building the Web Application: Feature

Scan Annotation

Our CAST web platform allows clinicians and vendors to digitally manage their orders. A unique feature we have is the ability to add, view, edit, and annotate scans with our scan previewer. Comments are also available to ensure clinicians and vendors can collaborate easily.

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, AngularJS
